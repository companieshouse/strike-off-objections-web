Object to a company being struck off

Use this service to tell us why a limited company should not be removed from the Companies House register.

You can only object to a company being struck off after the notice has been posted in The Gazette.

Companies House must receive the objection at least 2 weeks before the notice expiry date.

This service takes around 15 minutes.
